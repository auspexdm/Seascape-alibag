Getting Here

On the Revdanda–Murud road

The Seascape sits at Korlai on the Revdanda–Murud road, south of Alibag town. From Mumbai, take the Mandwa ferry and drive down the coast, or drive the full route via Pen — either way, the last stretch runs along some of the prettiest shoreline in Maharashtra.
