Your Neighbourhood

Alibaug: More Than a Beach Town

Alibaug has been a retreat for Mumbaikars for generations — but those who look beyond the shore discover a world of sea forts, colonial history, coconut groves, local seafood, and quiet temple towns. Use Seascape as your base.

Beach

Alibaug Beach

📍 0.5 km · 5 min walk

The town's main beach — great for evening walks, horse rides, and watching the sun set over the sea. Kolaba Fort is visible at low tide.

Fort · Heritage

Kolaba Fort

📍 1 km · Accessible by foot at low tide

A 17th-century sea fort built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Reachable on foot during low tide or by boat. Alibaug's most iconic landmark.

Beach

Kashid Beach

📍 30 km · 45 min drive

One of Maharashtra's most beautiful beaches — white sand, clear water, and a peaceful atmosphere. The coconut grove behind the beach is stunning.

Fort · Heritage

Murud-Janjira Fort

📍 55 km · 1.5 hr drive

The only fort on India's west coast that was never captured. Reached by boat from Rajpuri Jetty — one of Maharashtra's greatest historical treasures.

Activity · Travel

Mandwa Jetty & Ferry

📍 15 km · 20 min drive

The catamaran ferry from Mandwa to Gateway of India is a scenic 50-minute crossing — the best way to arrive or depart Alibaug.

Beach · Nature

Kihim Beach

📍 12 km · 15 min drive

A quieter beach beloved for its mangroves and birdlife. Ideal for birdwatchers, photographers, and anyone wanting a crowd-free coastal experience.

Beach

Nagaon Beach

📍 8 km · 10 min drive

A long stretch of soft sand, perfect for sunset walks, beach sports, and fresh fried fish at the stalls nearby. Calmer than the main Alibaug beach.

Food · Seafood

Konkan Seafood Shacks

📍 1–3 km · 5–10 min

Bombil, prawns, crab, surmai in spicy Malvani masala. The beachside shacks serve some of the finest coastal food you'll find anywhere.

Activity · Adventure

Water Sports

📍 2 km · Nagaon Beach

Jet skiing, banana boat rides, parasailing, and kayaking. A great adrenaline morning before a slow afternoon poolside at Seascape.

Nature · Wildlife

Phansad Wildlife Sanctuary

📍 40 km · 55 min drive

A hidden gem — rich with peacocks, sambar deer, Indian gaur, and over 130 bird species. Best visited in the early morning.

Heritage · History

Chaul — The Lost Portuguese Town

📍 22 km · 30 min drive

Portuguese, Maratha, and British history layered into one quiet town. The Revdanda Fort ruins, old churches, and peaceful creek make for a fascinating half-day.

Temple · Spirituality

Harihareshwar Temple

📍 90 km · 2 hr drive

The Kashi of the Konkan — ancient, sacred, and surrounded by dramatic sea cliffs. A day trip through gorgeous coastal countryside.

From Our Team

Tips for Exploring Alibaug

The things the travel guides don't tell you — from the people who live here.

🌊 Tide Timing

Kolaba Fort is accessible on foot only during low tide — for about 2 hours. Ask us at reception for the daily tide chart before heading out.

🚗 Getting Around

Autos and rented two-wheelers are best for short trips. For Kashid or Murud, we can arrange trusted local cab drivers with prior notice.

🍤 What to Eat

Bombil fry, Malvani crab curry, Sol Kadhi, and a fresh coconut straight from the tree. Non-negotiable on any Konkan visit.

📅 Best Season

October to March — clear skies, calm seas, cool evenings. Monsoon turns the coast lush and green but most water activities are paused.
