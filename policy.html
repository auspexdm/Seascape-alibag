🕐 Timings

Check-In & Check-Out

Check-In: 2:00 PM  |  Check-Out: 11:00 AM

Early check-in (from 10:00 AM) and late check-out (until 2:00 PM) may be arranged subject to room availability and will be charged at 50% of the nightly rate per half-day.

All adult guests must present a valid government-issued photo ID at check-in — Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, or Driving Licence. This is mandatory under local hospitality regulations.

If you expect to arrive after 8:00 PM, please inform us in advance so we can prepare a proper welcome for you.

📋 Cancellations

Cancellation Policy

  • More than 14 days before arrival: Full refund, minus a ₹500 processing fee.
  • 7–14 days before arrival: 50% refund of the total booking amount.
  • Less than 7 days before arrival: No refund. The full advance is forfeited.
  • No-show: Full booking amount is non-refundable.

Peak Season (Oct–Jan & long weekends): Cancellations within 14 days are non-refundable. We recommend travel insurance during peak periods.

All cancellations must be communicated in writing via email to theseascapevillaandresort@gmail.com or via WhatsApp with written confirmation.

💳 Payment

Payment Terms

Advance: 50% of the total booking amount is required at time of booking to confirm your reservation.

Balance: The remaining 50% is due at check-in, before keys are issued.

Accepted Methods:

  • Bank Transfer — NEFT / IMPS / RTGS
  • UPI — Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM
  • Credit / Debit Cards — Visa, Mastercard, RuPay
  • Cash (at check-in only)

All rates are in INR and exclude 12% GST. A GST invoice is available on request. Seascape does not process payments through third-party platforms — all bookings are direct.

🏡 House Rules

House Rules

  • Seascape is a quiet retreat. Loud music and noise are not permitted after 10:00 PM.
  • This is a non-smoking property indoors. Smoking only in designated outdoor areas.
  • Unregistered visitors are not permitted inside the property after 9:00 PM.
  • Parties and events require prior written approval and may attract additional charges.
  • Any form of illegal activity will result in immediate eviction without refund.
  • Guests are responsible for the safety of their personal belongings.
  • Room capacity must not exceed the declared occupancy. Additional guests will be charged.
  • Please respect the privacy of other guests and the comfort of all staff.
🏊 Pool & Beach

Pool & Beach Policy

Pool Hours: 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Use of private pools beyond hours is at the guest's own risk.

  • Swim wear is mandatory in all pool areas. Street clothes are not permitted in pools.
  • Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult in all pool areas at all times.
  • Glass items and crockery are strictly prohibited in or around pool areas.
  • Seascape is not liable for any accidents or injury in pool or beach areas.
  • Beach access is available sunrise to sunset. Do not swim in rough sea conditions.
  • Beach loungers and umbrellas are complimentary for in-house guests.
🍽️ Dining

Dining & Food

Complimentary breakfast is included for the declared number of guests in all room bookings.

In-room dining and BBQ arrangements are available and must be pre-booked at least 4 hours in advance.

Outside food and alcohol may be brought in for personal use. A corkage fee of ₹500 per bottle applies to alcohol not purchased from our property.

Please inform us of any dietary preferences or allergies at the time of booking so we may plan your meals accordingly.

👨‍👩‍👧 Guests

Children & Pets

Children:

  • Children below 5 stay complimentary — no extra bed required.
  • Children 5–12 years: ₹800/night for an extra mattress.
  • Above 12 years are considered adults for billing purposes.

Pets:

  • Seascape is not currently a pet-friendly property.
  • Guests with medical/service animals should contact us before booking.
⚠️ Damage

Damage & Security Deposit

A refundable security deposit of ₹3,000 (standard rooms) or ₹5,000 (pool rooms) is collected at check-in and refunded within 24 hours of check-out after room inspection.

Damage to property, furniture, or fixtures caused by guests will be deducted from the deposit. If the damage exceeds the deposit, the balance is invoiced and payable immediately.

Excessive mess requiring special cleaning or loss of key cards may attract additional charges.

🔐 Privacy

Privacy Policy

Personal information collected at booking (name, contact, ID, payment details) is used solely for completing your reservation and providing services during your stay.

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law — such as local police verification as mandated for hospitality properties in Maharashtra.

CCTV cameras are present in all common areas and property exteriors for guest and staff safety. No cameras are present in any private rooms or bathrooms.

For privacy-related queries, contact us at theseascapevillaandresort@gmail.com.
