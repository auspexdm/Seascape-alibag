Our Story

Born of the Konkan Shore

"A retreat designed not to impress — but to restore. The sea does the rest."

Seascape was conceived as an act of love for the Alibaug coastline — a place where the textures of contemporary design meet the raw, unhurried beauty of the Konkan sea. Every room frames the ocean. Every corner invites rest.

Whether you arrive for a weekend or a week, you'll leave a little lighter, a little more yourself — salt air in your hair and the sound of waves still echoing.
