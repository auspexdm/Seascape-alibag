Our Story

Built into the Konkan shoreline, not just near it

Nothing here is trying to impress you. It's trying to slow you down.

Seascape grew out of a long attachment to this stretch of Alibaug coast, where the design brief was less about adding features and more about getting out of the sea's way. Every room is angled toward the water; every material is chosen to weather well in salt air.

Stay a weekend or a week — you'll leave with sand still in your bag and the tide somewhere in the back of your head.

The Konkan Coast

Alibaug & beyond

Seascape sits at the centre of one of India's most underrated coastlines. Sea forts, coconut groves, fresh Konkan seafood, and centuries of maritime history are all within a short drive.

Kolaba Fort, Kashid Beach, Murud–Janjira, and the Mandwa ferry crossing are each reachable in an afternoon — Seascape is simply the best place to come back to.
